Run a two-good economy — first alone, then with trade

You manage Home, which makes Cloth and Wheat with 100 workers. Each round has two phases:

Phase 1 — Autarky: no trade. Allocate workers between the goods; your citizens consume exactly what you produce. Their happiness is highest with a balanced basket (utility = √(Cloth × Wheat)).

Phase 2 — Open to trade: a world market appears with a fixed world price. Re-allocate workers (hint: think about what Home is relatively better at) and export one good for the other.

Your score is the share of the possible gains from trade you actually capture, averaged over 3 rounds with different technologies. Doing no better than autarky scores 0 for the round — full specialization in the right good plus balanced trading scores 100%.
