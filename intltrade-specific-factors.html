Trade creates winners and losers. Which will you be?

The economy of Aldoria has two industries: Manufactures (uses capital + labor) and Food (uses land + labor). You will be randomly cast as a capitalist, a landowner, or a worker. Capital and land are stuck in their industries; only workers can move between them.

Over 5 rounds, world trade shocks hit and the parliament votes on trade policy. Each round you choose a position and may spend limited political capital to swing the vote. Policy changes goods prices — and the specific-factors model decides what happens to your real income.

Score = your final real income relative to the best you could have achieved in your role. Choosing what's good for the country is not always what's good for you — noticing that tension is the point of Chapter 3.
